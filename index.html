About Me

I'm currently a Master's student in IT Engineering at the University of Tehran with a strong passion for Large Language Models, Machine Behavior, and Social Intelligence.

During my Bachelor's in Industrial Engineering, I discovered my enthusiasm for computer science and started learning programming, particularly with Python. This passion motivated me to change my path, and after achieving rank 14 in the national entrance exam, I successfully transitioned to IT Engineering for my Master's degree at the University of Tehran.

Currently, I'm conducting research on Machine Behavior and Social Intelligence as a Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Azadeh Shakery and Dr. Yadollah Yaghoobzadeh in the Intelligent Information Systems Laboratory at the University of Tehran.

My goal is to innovate AI-powered solutions using NLP and LLMs that enhance Machine Behavior and Social Intelligence, contributing to solving complex challenges at the intersection of intelligent interaction and socially-aware AI.
