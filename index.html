About Me

I'm currently a Master's student in IT Engineering at the University of Tehran with a strong passion for Large Language Models, Machine Behavior and conversation.

During my Bachelor's in Industrial Engineering, I discovered my enthusiasm for computer science and started learning programming, particularly with Python. This passion motivated me to change my path, and after achieving rank 14 in the national entrance exam, I successfully transitioned to IT Engineering for my Master's degree in University Of Tehran.

Currently, I'm conducting research on Machine Behavior and Conversational Systems as a Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Azadeh Shakery in the Intelligent Information Systems Laboratory at the University of Tehran.

My goal is to innovate in AI-powered solutions using NLP and LLMs for Machine Behavior and to contribute to solving complex challenges in this domain.
